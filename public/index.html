Our Three Pillars

The Canadian Paediatric Society specializes in three key areas of paediatrics:

Public Policy Advocacy

The Canadian Paediatric Society produces information and resources on many child and youth health issues. Throughout this website, you’ll find position statements, advocacy tools, parent education resources, professional development opportunities and much more. In this section, we bring them together into several key focus areas.

Knowledge Translation

Canadian Paediatric Society position statements and practice points have clinical guidance and policy recommendations for paediatricians, family physicians, nurses, government officials and others. They also include links to other relevant documents and resources, including information for parents.

NRP: Neonatal Resuscitation Program

The Neonatal Resuscitation Program is an educational program that introduces the concepts and skills of neonatal resuscitation.

In Canada, NRP is administered by the Canadian Paediatric Society and is designed to teach individuals and teams who may be required to resuscitate newborn babies. NRP course content is evidence-based and is delivered across Canada in both English and French. Learn more about the Neonatal Resuscitation Program.
