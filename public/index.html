Measuring in support of early childhood development

In this statement, the Canadian Paediatric Society calls on federal and provincial/territorial governments to measure and monitor the developmental progress of children in Canada, which can vary widely among communities and demographic groups.

What We Do

Public Policy Advocacy

We advocate to all levels of government for legislation, programs, and policy that help support the health and well-being of children and youth.

Knowledge Translation

To ensure that health professionals, parents and others who care for children and youth are able to make decisions and take action that is based on the best available evidence, we develop programs, tools and resources.

Clinical Care

Our position statements, practice points, clinical tools, and educational resources are designed to ensure that paediatricians, family physicians, nurses, and others can deliver the best possible care to children and youth.

Contraceptive care for Canadian youth

This statement endorses LARCs as the first-line option for contraception for Canadian youth, while emphasizing that providers must collaborate with youth to select a contraceptive method that is acceptable, safe, effective and practical for them.
